# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2025**

StudioDESK ("the App") is operated by Shantel Bradford. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

---

## What We Collect

### Account data
When you create an account, we collect your **email address** via Supabase Auth. This is used solely for authentication.

### Session data
During a collaboration session, audio recordings, region data, and project state are transmitted between session participants in real-time. Audio recordings may be temporarily stored in Supabase Storage to allow engineers and artists to access them during a session.

### Usage data
We do not collect analytics, tracking cookies, or behavioral data.

---

## How We Use Your Data

- **Authentication** — Your email is used to log you in and identify your session.
- **Real-time collaboration** — Session state (transport position, track changes, RPC commands) is broadcast over Supabase Realtime channels to the other participant in your room.
- **Audio storage** — Recordings you make are stored in Supabase Storage under your account and associated with your room code.

We do not sell, share, or license your data to third parties.

Account data is retained until you delete your account. Recordings stored in Supabase Storage can be deleted by you at any time from the Media Pool panel.
